About Me

I’m a freelance videographer passionate about capturing unforgettable moments and telling compelling stories. My work spans a wide range of creative projects, from the adrenaline-filled action of sports highlights to visually striking music videos. I specialize in producing high-impact content tailored for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, helping musicians, athletes, and brands amplify their presence and connect authentically with their audiences. Beyond videography, I thrive in the exciting world of marketing. I design and execute innovative brand activations that leave lasting impressions, whether through guerrilla sampling campaigns, alcohol tastings, or interactive brand experiences at music festivals, vegan fairs and other events. My work blends creativity with strategy, delivering results that captivate and engage. Every project I take on reflects my dedication and commitment to bringing visions to life.
